Do I need any technical knowledge? ▼
Zero. We handle everything technical. You just tell us your goals and we build it. You'll get a simple dashboard to manage your tools with no coding required.
How fast will I see results? ▼
Most clients see their first new leads within 7 days of launch. SEO takes 45–90 days for full impact, but we set up quick-win funnels in parallel so you get results immediately.
What if I want to cancel? ▼
Cancel anytime — no lock-in contracts. You keep everything we built. The website, chatbot, automations — all yours. We just stop the monthly maintenance.
Is this built specifically for Canadian businesses? ▼
Yes. We focus exclusively on Canadian small businesses — especially Ontario. Local SEO, Canadian payment processors, HST compliance, Google Maps optimization for your city. We know this market.
What industries do you work with? ▼
Trades (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing), health & wellness, restaurants & food service, professional services (lawyers, accountants), retail, and real estate. If you serve local customers, we can help.
How is Momentum AI different from an agency? ▼
Traditional agencies charge $5,000–$30,000 upfront and take months to deliver. We're month-to-month, deliver in 7–14 days, and every service is tied to a specific business outcome. We're your ongoing AI partner, not a one-time project.
